Audio Recorder

The design of a basic audio recorder was broken down into detailed descriptions of its hardware and software design. The method of powering the system and distributing the power was documented, along with all necessary calculations to implement a similar device in a technical report. The device's performance was analyzed and checked against a list of operational requirements and it was concluded that the device performed adequately within these specifications, although there were certain adaptations that could be made to improve device performance and practical usability.

This project made use of various skills such as embedded programming in the STM environment, circuit construction, debugging and evaluation.
