Mostly Printed CNC

This project involved the design of a simple desktop CNC machine. The machine is expected to use a Dremel 3000 rotary tool as the spindle. The Y-axis will be driven by GT2 timing belts, with specific focus placed on reducing the length of belt under tension. This will limit the stretching of the belts and reduce the re-tensioning requirement of the system.

To date, this project has made use of various skills such as 3D design and dynamic RoM modelling, and will continue to use practical skills for the fabrication of the parts, assembly and testing. Furthermore, this project will require programming a GRBL controller, and generating G-code commands.
